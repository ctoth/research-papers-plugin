# Citations

## Key Citations for Follow-up
- Kiritchenko, S., et al. (2010). ExaCT: automatic extraction of clinical trial characteristics from journal publications. One of the closest included systems to end-to-end full-text trial-field extraction. *(pp.8, 15)*
- Marshall, I.J., et al. (2014). Automatic extraction of risk of bias from clinical trial reports. Directly relevant to review-methodology field extraction beyond simple PICO tagging. *(pp.8, 15)*
- Tsafnat, G., et al. (2014). Systematic review automation technologies. A broader review of the full review pipeline that this paper uses as context. *(p.12, 15)*
- Thomas, J., et al. (2011). The EPPI-Reviewer text mining tools. Relevant for situating data extraction inside the larger review workflow. *(p.12, 15)*
- Wallace, B.C., et al. (2012). Semi-automated screening of biomedical citations for systematic reviews. Useful comparison point for neighboring review-automation tasks. *(p.12, 15)*
- Cohen, A.M., et al. (2013). An evidence-based medicine information system. Relevant to the paper's discussion of broader clinical evidence retrieval and use. *(p.15)*
